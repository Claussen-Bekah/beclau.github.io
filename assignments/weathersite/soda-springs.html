57th Annual Rodeo Success

By Mayor Fielding

I'd like to make a big shoutout to all those who worked so hard and made the 57th Annual Soda Springs Rodeo such a massive success. Thank you especially to the Christensen and Yost families who catered the event. If you didn't get a scone, you missed out big time!

And, of course, congratulations to our winners: Dusty Smith, Alan Big, Daisy Applegate, and Tiberius White. Dusty [pictured] wowed us with her athleticism atop her horse, Winnie, as she finished our course in record time. Alan sat atop his bucking bronco for an astounding 10 seconds, bringing the audience to their feet.

And we've got to mention our littlest participants: Daisy and Tiberius. Both of these kids are only 7 years old, but they managed to get our hearts pumping in the mutton busting contest. I can't wait to see what they do next year! And, boy, do we have big plans for next year. Our theme for the rodeo this year was "Forward Together" and here in the mayoral office,we can't wait to see the strides we will make as a town in the year to come!
